How it works

Dandy Bot fetches the current Twisted Board status every minute from a website, compares the latest board data, and notifies your Discord channel the moment something changes. This ensures your community always has the freshest, actionable information with minimal setup.

⚡ Instant Alerts

Receive updates within minutes of board rotation, no manual checking required.

🔧 One-click Setup

Invite the bot, authorized a channel, and you're ready. Works without extra permissions.

📢 Clear Formatting

Structured embeds and plain text output ensure everyone in your server understands updates at a glance.

🛡️ Stable Monitoring

24/7 uptime and retry logic built in for reliability during transient Roblox or network outages.

FAQ

How do I install it?

Click ‘Invite Dandy Bot’, select your server, and grant basic permissions it requests. Then configure a channel with !setchannel if needed.

How often is the board update check?

Typically every 1 minute.

Can I choose the language of alerts?

Language options are planned for a future release; for now updates are in English only.
